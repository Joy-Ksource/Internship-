MS Dhoni Tribute Page

Introduction

Mahendra Singh Dhoni, commonly known as MS Dhoni, is a former Indian international cricketer and captain of the Indian national team. He is considered one of the greatest wicket-keeper batsmen in the history of cricket.

Achievements

  • Led India to win the ICC T20 World Cup in 2007
  • Captained India to win the ICC Cricket World Cup in 2011
  • Guided India to the top of the Test rankings
  • Recipient of several awards including the Rajiv Gandhi Khel Ratna and Padma Shri

Retirement

MS Dhoni announced his retirement from international cricket on 15th August 2020. He left behind a legacy of leadership, calmness, and match-winning performances that made him one of the most beloved cricketers in the world.
